About This Project

This project, "CyberShield," aims to provide a comprehensive suite of web vulnerability tools, including phishing URL detection, vulnerability scanning, and cyber attack prediction. With the increasing frequency of cyber threats, it's essential to have reliable tools that help identify vulnerabilities and predict possible attacks. This platform allows users to easily scan URLs for vulnerabilities, detect phishing attempts, and predict potential cyber attacks using machine learning models trained on historical data.

Our mission is to make web security accessible to everyone, from developers to organizations, ensuring a safer online experience. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, we aim to help you identify threats before they become real issues.
